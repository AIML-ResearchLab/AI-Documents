# 06 - Model Validation and Cross-Validation

## 6.1 Why Validation Matters
Reliable validation prevents overestimating model quality.

## 6.2 Validation Strategies
- holdout split
- k-fold cross-validation
- stratified/time-based splits

## 6.3 Leakage Controls
Keep feature fitting and preprocessing strictly within training folds.

## 6.4 Reporting
Always report mean and variability across folds.
