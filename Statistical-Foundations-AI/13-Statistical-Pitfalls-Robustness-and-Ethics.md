# 13 - Statistical Pitfalls, Robustness, and Ethics

## 13.1 Common Statistical Pitfalls
- Data leakage
- p-hacking
- target leakage
- overfitting to validation set
- cherry-picked metrics

## 13.2 Robustness Practices
- Cross-validation
- Sensitivity analysis
- Stress testing on edge cohorts
- Repeated runs with uncertainty intervals

## 13.3 Distribution Shift
Monitor covariate shift, label shift, and concept drift post-deployment.

## 13.4 Fairness and Bias
Evaluate model performance across demographic and operational groups.

## 13.5 Ethics and Governance
- Transparent metric reporting
- Explain assumptions and limitations
- Escalate high-impact decisions to human oversight

## 13.6 Reliability Reporting Standard
Every model report should include:
- metric point estimate
- uncertainty interval
- known failure modes
- data coverage limits
