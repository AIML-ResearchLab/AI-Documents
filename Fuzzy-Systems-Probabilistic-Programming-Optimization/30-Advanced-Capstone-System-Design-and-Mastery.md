# 30 - Advanced Capstone: System Design and Mastery

## 30.1 Objective
Design a full decision intelligence system combining fuzzy reasoning, probabilistic inference, and constrained optimization.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
- architecture and dataflow design
- fuzzy/probabilistic/optimization specs
- evaluation and robustness plan
- deployment and rollback strategy
- monitoring and governance checklist

## 30.3 Rubric
- technical correctness
- uncertainty calibration quality
- optimization effectiveness under constraints
- operational readiness
- documentation quality

## 30.4 Suggested Capstones
- risk-aware triage engine
- robust resource allocation planner
- compliance-constrained decision support system
