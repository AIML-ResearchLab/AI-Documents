# 09 - Inference Basics: MCMC and Variational Methods

## 9.1 MCMC
Sample from posterior via Markov chain transitions.

## 9.2 Variational Inference
Optimize approximate posterior for speed and scalability.

## 9.3 Tradeoffs
MCMC: often more accurate but slower. VI: faster but approximation-biased.

## 9.4 Real-Time Example
Use VI for low-latency online updates and MCMC for periodic calibration.
