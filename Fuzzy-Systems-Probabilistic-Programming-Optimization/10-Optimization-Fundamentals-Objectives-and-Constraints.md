# 10 - Optimization Fundamentals: Objectives and Constraints

## 10.1 Problem Definition
Minimize or maximize objective subject to constraints.

## 10.2 Components
- decision variables
- objective function
- equality/inequality constraints

## 10.3 AI Relevance
Optimization converts model predictions into optimal actions.

## 10.4 Real-Time Example
Optimize staffing plan given predicted call demand and labor constraints.
