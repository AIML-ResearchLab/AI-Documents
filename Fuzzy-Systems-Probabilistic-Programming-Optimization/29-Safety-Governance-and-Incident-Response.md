# 29 - Safety, Governance, and Incident Response

## 29.1 Governance Requirements
Traceability of rules, priors, inference settings, and optimization constraints.

## 29.2 Safety Risks
constraint violations, calibration drift, and optimization side effects.

## 29.3 Incident Runbook
1. detect issue
2. activate safe fallback policy
3. diagnose model/constraint drift
4. patch and validate

## 29.4 Real-Time Example
Constraint breach triggers rollback to last validated decision policy.
