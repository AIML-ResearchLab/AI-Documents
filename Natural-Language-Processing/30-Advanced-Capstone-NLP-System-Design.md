# 30 - Advanced Capstone: NLP System Design

## 30.1 Objective
Design a production NLP system from ingestion to monitoring with governance controls.

## 30.2 Deliverables
- architecture document
- training/evaluation protocol
- release and rollback plan
- monitoring and incident runbook
- safety and compliance checklist

## 30.3 Rubric
- technical correctness
- robustness and safety
- operational maturity
- cost and latency efficiency
- documentation quality

## 30.4 Suggested Capstones
- enterprise support copilot
- policy QA assistant
- multilingual summarization system
- compliance extraction pipeline
