# 01 - Program Overview and Speech Analytics Lifecycle

## 1.1 Goal
Build end-to-end speech analytics capability from raw audio ingestion to operational insights.

## 1.2 Lifecycle
1. ingest audio
2. preprocess and validate
3. transcribe and diarize
4. analyze sentiment/compliance/intent
5. deploy and monitor

## 1.3 Principles
- reproducibility
- quality controls
- privacy and compliance
- operational reliability
