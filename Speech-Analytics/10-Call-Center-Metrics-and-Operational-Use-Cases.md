# 10 - Call Center Metrics and Operational Use Cases

## 10.1 Core Metrics
AHT, FCR, CSAT proxy signals, escalation rate, transfer rate.

## 10.2 Speech Analytics Use Cases
- compliance monitoring
- root-cause trend detection
- churn-risk signals
- agent performance support

## 10.3 Operational Integration
Connect analytics outputs to QA systems, CRM, and workforce tooling.

## 10.4 Real-Time Example
Auto-detect policy disclosure omissions in regulated support calls.
