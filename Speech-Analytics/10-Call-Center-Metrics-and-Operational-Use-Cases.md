# 10 - Call-Center Metrics and Operational Use Cases

## Objective
Connect speech analytics to measurable business outcomes.

## Core Contact-Center Metrics
- Average handle time (AHT)
- First contact resolution (FCR)
- Transfer rate
- Hold time and silence ratio
- Compliance pass rate
- Customer sentiment trajectory

## High-Value Use Cases
- Automated QA scoring
- Compliance detection and audit support
- Agent coaching and skill-gap analysis
- Escalation prevention and churn-risk prediction

## Real-Time Example
A telecom support operation uses live script-compliance detection to reduce legal exposure and cut post-call audit effort by prioritizing only high-risk calls.
