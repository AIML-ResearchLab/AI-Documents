# 13 - Positive Definite Matrices and Quadratic Forms

## 13.1 Quadratic Form
`x^T A x` describes curvature geometry.

## 13.2 Positive Definite (PD)
Matrix `A` is PD if `x^T A x > 0` for all nonzero `x`.

## 13.3 Positive Semidefinite (PSD)
`x^T A x >= 0`.

## 13.4 Optimization Link
Hessian PSD/PD properties determine convexity and optimization stability.

## 13.5 AI Use Cases
- covariance matrices
- kernel matrices
- second-order optimization analysis
