# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Linear Algebra System Design

## 30.1 Objective
Design a production-grade AI workflow with explicit linear algebra decisions and numerical safeguards.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
- mathematical design spec
- decomposition and solver choices
- stability/risk analysis
- benchmark and performance report
- incident and rollback plan

## 30.3 Evaluation Rubric
- correctness of mathematical approach
- numerical stability and robustness
- scalability and efficiency
- clarity of documentation
- operational readiness

## 30.4 Suggested Capstones
- large-scale recommendation factorization
- embedding retrieval platform
- graph-based prediction pipeline
- low-rank compression for model serving
