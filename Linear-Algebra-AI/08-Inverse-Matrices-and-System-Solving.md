# 08 - Inverse Matrices and System Solving

## 8.1 Matrix Inverse
`A^{-1}` satisfies `AA^{-1} = I`.

## 8.2 Solving Systems
`x = A^{-1}b` mathematically, but in practice use decomposition solvers.

## 8.3 Why Not Explicit Inverse
Explicit inversion is slower and less stable than solving linear systems directly.

## 8.4 Decomposition Alternatives
- LU decomposition
- QR decomposition
- Cholesky for SPD matrices

## 8.5 AI Relevance
Stable solvers are critical in regression and Kalman-style systems.
