# 22 - Iterative Solvers and Krylov Subspace Methods

## 22.1 Why Iterative Solvers
For very large systems, direct factorization is expensive.

## 22.2 Common Methods
- gradient descent for least squares
- conjugate gradient (SPD systems)
- GMRES/BiCGSTAB for general systems

## 22.3 Preconditioning
Good preconditioners drastically improve convergence speed.

## 22.4 Stopping Criteria
Use residual thresholds and max iteration limits.

## 22.5 AI Relevance
Large-scale optimization and inverse problems rely on iterative methods.
