# 10 - Orthogonality, Projections, and Gram-Schmidt

## 10.1 Orthogonality
Vectors are orthogonal if dot product is zero.

## 10.2 Projection
Project vector `x` onto subspace basis to isolate explainable component.

## 10.3 Gram-Schmidt
Transforms independent vectors into orthonormal basis.

## 10.4 QR Decomposition
Gram-Schmidt underlies QR methods used in stable solving and least squares.

## 10.5 AI Relevance
Orthogonal components improve interpretability and numerical behavior.
