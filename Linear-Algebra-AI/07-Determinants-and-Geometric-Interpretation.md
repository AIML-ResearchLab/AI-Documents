# 07 - Determinants and Geometric Interpretation

## 7.1 Determinant Meaning
`det(A)` measures volume scaling of transformation `A`.

## 7.2 Invertibility Test
`det(A) != 0` implies matrix is invertible.

## 7.3 Significance of Sign
Sign reflects orientation flip or preservation.

## 7.4 Practical Note
Determinants are often numerically unstable for large matrices; prefer decomposition-based checks.

## 7.5 AI Relevance
Log-determinants appear in probabilistic models and covariance computations.
