# 09 - Reranking, Relevance, and Retrieval Quality

## 9.1 Why Reranking
First-pass ANN retrieval is fast; rerankers improve final precision.

## 9.2 Reranking Models
Cross-encoders and task-specific rankers reorder candidates by deeper relevance.

## 9.3 Retrieval Features
Use semantic score, lexical score, freshness, and authority signals.

## 9.4 Quality Governance
Track failure slices by intent, language, and domain type.

## 9.5 Real-Time Example
Reranker reduces irrelevant document citations in an enterprise Q&A assistant.
