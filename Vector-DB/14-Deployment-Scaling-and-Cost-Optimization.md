# 14 - Deployment, Scaling, and Cost Optimization

## 14.1 Deployment Models
Choose managed, self-hosted, or hybrid based on compliance and operations.

## 14.2 Scaling Strategy
Scale read and write paths independently where supported.

## 14.3 Cost Levers
Optimize dimension size, top-k, index type, and storage class.

## 14.4 Reliability Engineering
Use backups, replication, disaster recovery, and staged rollouts.

## 14.5 Real-Time Example
Vector workload migrates to disk-based indexing to cut memory cost at scale.
