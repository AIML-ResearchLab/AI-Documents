# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Production AI System Design

## 30.1 Objective
Design and document a production-grade AI system with full maintenance plan.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
- architecture and deployment plan
- validation and release strategy
- monitoring and incident runbooks
- governance/security/compliance controls
- cost and capacity plan

## 30.3 Rubric
- technical correctness
- reliability and safety
- operational readiness
- cost-performance efficiency
- documentation quality

## 30.4 Suggested Capstones
- enterprise support copilot platform
- fraud scoring system with continuous retraining
- policy-compliant agentic workflow orchestration
