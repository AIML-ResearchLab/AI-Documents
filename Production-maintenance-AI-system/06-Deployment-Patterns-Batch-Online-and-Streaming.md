# 06 - Deployment Patterns: Batch, Online, and Streaming

## 6.1 Batch Deployment
Best for periodic, non-latency-critical workloads.

## 6.2 Online Deployment
Best for low-latency, user-facing inference.

## 6.3 Streaming Deployment
Best for continuous event-based decisions.
