# 08 - Artifacts, Results, Status, and Output Packaging

## 8.1 Artifact Model
Artifacts represent durable outputs such as summaries, files, plans, or structured records.

## 8.2 Output Lifecycle
Artifacts may be partial during execution and finalized at completion.

## 8.3 Status Signaling
Result status and metadata communicate completeness and confidence.

## 8.4 Real-Time Example
A legal-review agent emits draft clauses first, then final approved artifacts.
