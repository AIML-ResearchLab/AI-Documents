# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Big Data System Design

## 30.1 Objective
Design a production-grade big data analytics platform with reliability and governance.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
- architecture and pipeline design
- quality and governance controls
- cost and performance plan
- monitoring and incident runbooks

## 30.3 Rubric
technical correctness, scalability, reliability, governance maturity, and documentation quality.

## 30.4 Suggested Capstones
real-time fraud analytics, customer intelligence platform, or compliance analytics backbone.
