# 23 - Policy, Compliance, Privacy, and Human Governance

## 23.1 Policy Stack
Define policy layers:
- legal/regulatory constraints
- organization policy
- product-specific policy
- runtime exceptions and escalation

## 23.2 Privacy Controls
- data minimization in prompts
- PII masking/redaction
- retention windows for logs and traces

## 23.3 Compliance-by-Design Prompts
Include explicit compliance checks in instructions and validators.

## 23.4 Human-in-the-Loop Gates
Mandatory review for high-risk outputs:
- legal advice
- medical/financial decisions
- irreversible account actions

## 23.5 Auditability
Store:
- prompt version
- context sources
- policy decisions
- reviewer actions

## 23.6 Real-Time Example
Insurance claim assistant:
- auto-draft allowed
- payout recommendation requires human approval
- full audit trail captured

## 23.7 Governance Checklist
- role ownership defined
- escalation SLA defined
- policy updates versioned and tested
