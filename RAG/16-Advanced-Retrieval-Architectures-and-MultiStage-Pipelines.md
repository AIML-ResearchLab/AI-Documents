# 16 - Advanced Retrieval Architectures and Multi-Stage Pipelines

## 16.1 Multi-Stage Design
candidate generation -> reranking -> evidence filtering -> contextual synthesis.

## 16.2 Benefits
improves relevance under strict latency and cost constraints.

## 16.3 Operational Tradeoffs
more stages increase complexity and observability requirements.

## 16.4 Real-Time Example
Two-stage retrieval reduces unsupported answers in regulated domain assistant.
