# 23 - Hallucination Control, Verification, and Faithfulness

## 23.1 Faithfulness Objective
generated statements must be supported by retrieved evidence.

## 23.2 Verification Layers
claim extraction, evidence matching, and unsupported-claim rejection.

## 23.3 Mitigation
uncertainty signaling, refusal policies, and stricter evidence thresholds.

## 23.4 Real-Time Example
Verifier blocks answer when no evidence supports requested claim.
