# 12 - Evaluation Metrics for RAG Systems

## 12.1 Retrieval Metrics
recall@k, precision@k, MRR, and NDCG.

## 12.2 Generation Metrics
faithfulness, groundedness, completeness, and schema pass rate.

## 12.3 End-to-End Metrics
task success rate, latency, cost per request, and human acceptance.
