# 04 - Chunking Strategies and Metadata Design

## 4.1 Chunking Goals
maximize semantic coherence while fitting retrieval/query constraints.

## 4.2 Strategies
fixed, semantic, hierarchical, and overlap-based chunking.

## 4.3 Metadata
store source id, title, timestamp, section path, trust level, and access policy.

## 4.4 Real-Time Example
Hybrid chunking improves recall for policy Q&A with long legal sections.
