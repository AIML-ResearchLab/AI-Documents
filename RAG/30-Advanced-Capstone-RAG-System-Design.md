# 30 - Advanced Capstone: RAG System Design

## 30.1 Objective
Design a production-grade RAG system with robust retrieval, grounded generation, and operational controls.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
- architecture and dataflow design
- retrieval/generation evaluation report
- safety and governance plan
- deployment, monitoring, and rollback strategy

## 30.3 Rubric
technical quality, faithfulness, reliability, efficiency, and documentation completeness.

## 30.4 Suggested Capstones
enterprise policy assistant, support troubleshooting copilot, or compliance evidence assistant.
