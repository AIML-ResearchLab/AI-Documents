# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Computer Vision System Design

## 30.1 Objective
Design a production CV system with data, model, deployment, and governance plan.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
- architecture document
- training/evaluation protocol
- deployment and rollback plan
- monitoring and alert specification
- risk and compliance checklist

## 30.3 Rubric
- technical quality
- robustness and safety
- scalability and latency
- operational maturity
- documentation completeness

## 30.4 Suggested Capstones
- smart surveillance analytics
- industrial defect inspection
- medical imaging triage
- retail shelf intelligence
