# 02 - Image Representation and Color Spaces

## 2.1 Image Basics
Images are matrices/tensors of pixel intensities.

## 2.2 Channels and Formats
- grayscale
- RGB
- RGBA

## 2.3 Color Spaces
- RGB
- HSV
- YCbCr/Lab (task-dependent)

## 2.4 Practical Rule
Use color space matching your task and lighting variation characteristics.
