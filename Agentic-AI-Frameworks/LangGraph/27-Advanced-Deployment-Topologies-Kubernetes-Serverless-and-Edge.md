# 27 - Advanced Deployment Topologies: Kubernetes, Serverless, and Edge

## 27.1 Topology Selection
Choose deployment model by latency, scale, and governance constraints.

## 27.2 Kubernetes Pattern
Use containerized graph workers with autoscaling and policy controls.

## 27.3 Serverless and Edge
Use event-driven runtimes for bursty loads and low-latency regional serving.

## 27.4 Reliability Design
Implement health checks, retries, and regional failover plans.

## 27.5 Real-Time Example
Global support graph serves region-local requests from edge workers.
