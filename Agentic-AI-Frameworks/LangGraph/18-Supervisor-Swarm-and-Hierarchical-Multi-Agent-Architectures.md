# 18 - Supervisor, Swarm, and Hierarchical Multi-Agent Architectures

## 18.1 Supervisor Pattern
Central coordinator assigns subtasks and consolidates outputs.

## 18.2 Swarm Pattern
Peer agents collaborate with lightweight coordination for adaptive discovery.

## 18.3 Hierarchical Pattern
Nested teams support complex domain decomposition at scale.

## 18.4 Governance Controls
Limit delegation depth, tool scope, and cross-agent data exposure.

Incident commander agent orchestrates diagnosis swarm and escalation hierarchy.
