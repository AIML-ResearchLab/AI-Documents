# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Design a Production LangGraph Platform

## 30.1 Capstone Goal
Design a production LangGraph platform with reliability, governance, and scalability controls.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
Architecture diagram, state model, approval design, evaluation plan, and SLO framework.

## 30.3 Evaluation Rubric
Assess reliability, safety, cost-efficiency, and operational maturity.

## 30.4 Failure Simulation
Include outage recovery, rollback steps, and post-incident improvement plan.

## 30.5 Real-Time Example
Capstone system automates enterprise support with auditable approvals and durable execution.
