# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Design a Production CrewAI Platform

## 30.1 Capstone Objective
Design a full production platform using CrewAI agents, crews, flows, and governance controls.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
Architecture diagram, process strategy, memory/knowledge plan, evaluation framework, and SLO model.

## 30.3 Evaluation Rubric
Score reliability, safety, cost-efficiency, and operational maturity.

## 30.4 Operational Scenario
Include failure simulation, rollback playbook, and incident communication plan.

## 30.5 Real-Time Example
Capstone solution automates customer operations with audited approvals and event-driven escalation.
