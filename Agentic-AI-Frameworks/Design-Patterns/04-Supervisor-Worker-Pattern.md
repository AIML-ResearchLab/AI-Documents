# 04 - Supervisor-Worker Pattern

## 4.1 Pattern Intent
Use a central supervisor to assign and coordinate specialized workers.

## 4.2 Supervisor Role
Route tasks, enforce policy, and synthesize final outputs.

## 4.3 Worker Role
Perform domain-specific tasks with bounded autonomy.

## 4.4 Governance Benefit
Centralized control simplifies policy and approval enforcement.

## 4.5 Real-Time Example
Support supervisor delegates billing, technical, and policy tasks to worker agents.
