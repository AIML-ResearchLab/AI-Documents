# 17 - Swarm and Market-Based Coordination Pattern

## 17.1 Pattern Intent
Enable decentralized agent collaboration for exploratory problem spaces.

## 17.2 Swarm Mechanics
Agents share partial solutions and iterate toward consensus or best candidate.

## 17.3 Market-Based Control
Use scoring, bidding, or utility signals to allocate tasks.

## 17.4 Governance Needs
Require strong safeguards to prevent unbounded collaboration loops.

## 17.5 Real-Time Example
Research swarm explores multiple hypotheses and selects best-evidence synthesis.
