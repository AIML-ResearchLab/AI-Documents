# 10 - Human-in-the-Loop and Approval Gate Pattern

## 10.1 Pattern Intent
Insert human judgment before high-risk actions.

## 10.2 Trigger Conditions
Use confidence thresholds, policy flags, and action criticality.

## 10.3 Approval Workflow
Pause, present context, collect decision, and resume safely.

## 10.4 Audit Requirements
Log approver identity, decision rationale, and timestamps.

## 10.5 Real-Time Example
Procurement workflow pauses before contract-signing action for legal approval.
