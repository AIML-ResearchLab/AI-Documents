# 27 - Multi-Tenant Isolation and Governance Pattern

## 27.1 Pattern Intent
Protect tenant boundaries across data, memory, and tool execution.

## 27.2 Isolation Methods
Use namespaces, scoped credentials, and policy-based access controls.

## 27.3 Governance Model
Apply tenant-level audit, retention, and policy override workflows.

## 27.4 Compliance Readiness
Map tenant controls to regional and regulatory obligations.

## 27.5 Real-Time Example
Platform prevents cross-tenant retrieval despite shared infrastructure.
