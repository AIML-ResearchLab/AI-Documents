# 18 - Blackboard and Shared Knowledge Space Pattern

## 18.1 Pattern Intent
Use a shared workspace where agents post intermediate findings.

## 18.2 Blackboard Model
Agents read/write standardized artifacts to coordinate asynchronously.

## 18.3 Consistency Controls
Apply versioning, conflict resolution, and provenance tracking.

## 18.4 Quality Benefits
Encourages explainable collaboration and reproducible outcomes.

## 18.5 Real-Time Example
Investigation agents share evidence graph in blackboard before final risk decision.
