# 22 - Policy-Governed Action Execution Pattern

## 22.1 Pattern Intent
Wrap all side-effecting actions in policy and approval controls.

## 22.2 Policy Engine
Evaluate context, actor, and action risk before execution.

## 22.3 Enforcement Modes
Support allow, deny, transform, or escalate outcomes.

## 22.4 Audit Requirements
Record policy decisions and justifications for governance.

## 22.5 Real-Time Example
Procurement action is denied when approval chain is incomplete.
