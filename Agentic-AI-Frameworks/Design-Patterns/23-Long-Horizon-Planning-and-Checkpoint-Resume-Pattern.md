# 23 - Long-Horizon Planning and Checkpoint-Resume Pattern

## 23.1 Pattern Intent
Support workflows spanning hours or days with safe resumability.

## 23.2 Planning Structure
Break long goals into milestones with explicit checkpoints.

## 23.3 Resume Semantics
Resume from last verified checkpoint after interruption.

## 23.4 Failure Recovery
Use compensation paths for partially completed milestones.

## 23.5 Real-Time Example
Onboarding automation resumes from compliance checkpoint after maintenance outage.
