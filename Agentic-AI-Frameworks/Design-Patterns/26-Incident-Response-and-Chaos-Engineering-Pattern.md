# 26 - Incident Response and Chaos Engineering Pattern

## 26.1 Pattern Intent
Build operational resilience through planned failure testing.

## 26.2 Incident Lifecycle
Detect, triage, contain, recover, and review with runbook discipline.

## 26.3 Chaos Testing
Inject controlled failures in tools, models, and dependencies.

## 26.4 Learning Loop
Convert incident findings into test and policy improvements.

## 26.5 Real-Time Example
Chaos test simulates vector store outage and validates fallback retrieval path.
