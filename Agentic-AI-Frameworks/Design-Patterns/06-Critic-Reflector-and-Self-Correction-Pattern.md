# 06 - Critic, Reflector, and Self-Correction Pattern

## 6.1 Pattern Intent
Improve output quality through structured critique before final response.

## 6.2 Critic Role
Evaluate factuality, completeness, and policy compliance.

## 6.3 Reflection Loop
Revise outputs based on critique with bounded iteration depth.

## 6.4 Cost Controls
Limit loop count and use selective triggering for high-risk tasks only.

## 6.5 Real-Time Example
Contract summary is revised after critic flags missing liability clause.
