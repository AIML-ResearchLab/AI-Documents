# 01 - Layer 01: Business Objectives and Value Strategy

## 1.1 Purpose
Define measurable business outcomes the agentic platform must produce.

## 1.2 Core Decisions
Prioritize revenue, risk reduction, efficiency, and user-experience targets.

## 1.3 Design Artifacts
Value hypothesis, KPI tree, and success criteria per domain.

## 1.4 Failure Mode
No clear value target leads to technically correct but low-impact systems.

## 1.5 Real-Time Example
Support automation is scoped to cut ticket resolution time by 30%.
