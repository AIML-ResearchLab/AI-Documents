# 16 - Layer 16: Operations, SRE, and Lifecycle Management

## 16.1 Purpose
Operate agentic systems reliably through release, maintenance, and retirement.

## 16.2 Core Decisions
Define ownership, on-call rotations, and lifecycle governance.

## 16.3 Design Artifacts
Runbooks, change calendar, and deprecation strategy.

## 16.4 Failure Mode
No lifecycle discipline causes operational drift and unmanaged risk.

## 16.5 Real-Time Example
SRE workflow enforces canary rollout and auto-rollback on SLA breach.
