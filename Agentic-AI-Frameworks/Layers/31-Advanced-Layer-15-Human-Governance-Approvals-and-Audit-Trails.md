# 31 - Advanced Layer 15: Human Governance, Approvals, and Audit Trails

## 31.1 Purpose
Embed accountable human oversight into enterprise agent actions.

## 31.2 Core Decisions
Define approval roles, thresholds, and exception workflows.

## 31.3 Design Artifacts
Approval matrix, audit schema, and compliance evidence store.

## 31.4 Failure Mode
No audit trail undermines accountability and legal defensibility.

## 31.5 Real-Time Example
High-value procurement actions require two-level approval and full trace logs.
