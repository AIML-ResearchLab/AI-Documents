# 18 - Advanced Layer 02: Regulatory Controls and Compliance by Design

## 18.1 Purpose
Embed regulatory requirements into architecture rather than post-release patches.

## 18.2 Core Decisions
Map regulations to data handling, approvals, and model behavior controls.

## 18.3 Design Artifacts
Control matrix, compliance traces, and automated policy checks.

## 18.4 Failure Mode
Late compliance retrofits cause delays and legal exposure.

## 18.5 Real-Time Example
Healthcare workflows enforce jurisdiction-specific retention and masking policies.
