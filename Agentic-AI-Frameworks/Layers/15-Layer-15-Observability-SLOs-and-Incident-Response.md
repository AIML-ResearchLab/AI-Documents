# 15 - Layer 15: Observability, SLOs, and Incident Response

## 15.1 Purpose
Provide continuous visibility into behavior, reliability, and user impact.

## 15.2 Core Decisions
Define SLOs for latency, success rate, and safety outcomes.

## 15.3 Design Artifacts
Telemetry model, alerting rules, and incident runbooks.

## 15.4 Failure Mode
Poor observability delays detection and increases outage impact.

## 15.5 Real-Time Example
On-call detects rising tool timeouts and activates fallback mode.
