# 13 - Layer 13: Human Approval and Exception Handling

## 13.1 Purpose
Route high-risk decisions and unexpected failures to human supervision.

## 13.2 Core Decisions
Define interrupt triggers and escalation paths by risk tier.

## 13.3 Design Artifacts
Approval workflow, exception taxonomy, and fallback playbooks.

## 13.4 Failure Mode
No human override path increases operational and compliance risk.

## 13.5 Real-Time Example
Contract finalization pauses until legal approver signs off.
