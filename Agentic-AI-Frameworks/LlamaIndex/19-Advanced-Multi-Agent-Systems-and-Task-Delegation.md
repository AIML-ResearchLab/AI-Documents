# 19 - Advanced Multi-Agent Systems and Task Delegation

## 19.1 Topology Patterns
Use supervisor-worker, committee, and hierarchical delegation models.

## 19.2 Delegation Contracts
Pass structured context, confidence, and ownership metadata between agents.

## 19.3 Governance Controls
Limit delegation depth and enforce domain-specific permissions.

## 19.4 Failure Isolation
Contain sub-agent failures with fallback and escalation strategies.

## 19.5 Real-Time Example
Research coordinator delegates retrieval, validation, and synthesis to specialized agents.
