# 09 - Response Synthesis, Citations, and Groundedness

## 9.1 Synthesis Strategies
Use compact, refine, or tree-based response synthesis depending on context size.

## 9.2 Citation Design
Include source references to support traceability and trust.

## 9.3 Groundedness Checks
Detect unsupported claims and trigger re-retrieval where needed.

## 9.4 Quality Controls
Set confidence thresholds and answer refusal policies for weak evidence.

## 9.5 Real-Time Example
Compliance bot refuses answer when citations do not meet evidence threshold.
