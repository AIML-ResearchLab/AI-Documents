# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Design a Production LlamaIndex Platform

## 30.1 Capstone Goal
Design a production-grade LlamaIndex platform with retrieval, agents, and governance controls.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
Architecture diagram, ingestion/retrieval design, evaluation framework, and operational SLOs.

## 30.3 Evaluation Rubric
Assess reliability, faithfulness, scalability, and compliance readiness.

## 30.4 Failure Simulation
Include outage, rollback, and post-incident improvement plan.

## 30.5 Real-Time Example
Capstone team ships enterprise assistant with governed retrieval and auditable tool workflows.
