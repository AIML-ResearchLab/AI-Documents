# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Design a Production Google ADK Platform

## 30.1 Capstone Goal
Design a full production ADK platform with governance, reliability, and scale controls.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
Architecture diagram, agent topology, tool governance plan, evaluation framework, and SLOs.

## 30.3 Evaluation Rubric
Assess reliability, security, cost-efficiency, and operational maturity.

## 30.4 Failure Simulation
Include outage scenario, rollback process, and incident communication plan.

## 30.5 Real-Time Example
Capstone platform automates enterprise support with auditable approvals and resilient runtime behavior.
