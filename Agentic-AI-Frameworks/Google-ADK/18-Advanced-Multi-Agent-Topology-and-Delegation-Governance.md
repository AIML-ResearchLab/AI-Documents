# 18 - Advanced Multi-Agent Topology and Delegation Governance

## 18.1 Topology Patterns
Use supervisor-worker, mesh, and hub-and-spoke structures by workload complexity.

## 18.2 Delegation Contracts
Define handoff schema, confidence signals, and acceptance criteria.

## 18.3 Governance Controls
Limit delegation depth and enforce domain-based authorization.

## 18.4 Failure Isolation
Contain agent failures to sub-graphs to avoid cascade disruption.

## 18.5 Real-Time Example
Operations coordinator delegates incident diagnosis and remediation to specialized agents.
