# 06 - Multi-Agent Systems, Delegation, and Collaboration

## 6.1 Multi-Agent Value
Specialized agents improve quality for complex multi-step tasks.

## 6.2 Delegation Strategy
Assign subtasks to domain-specific agents with explicit handoff contracts.

## 6.3 Collaboration Controls
Bound turn counts, role permissions, and escalation rules.

## 6.4 Failure Handling
Fallback paths and coordinator agents prevent deadlock.

## 6.5 Real-Time Example
Travel assistant delegates pricing, policy, and itinerary generation to specialized agents.
