# 27 - Production Deployment Topologies and Operational Excellence

## 27.1 Topology Choices
Use monolithic, service-oriented, or event-driven deployment based on scale and governance needs.

## 27.2 Release Engineering
Apply canary rollout, feature flags, and automated rollback.

## 27.3 Operational SLOs
Track availability, latency, incident frequency, and recovery time metrics.

## 27.4 Runbook Discipline
Maintain runbooks for model outages, plugin failures, and policy incidents.

## 27.5 Real-Time Example
Production rollout auto-rolls back when hallucination and error rates exceed SLO thresholds.
