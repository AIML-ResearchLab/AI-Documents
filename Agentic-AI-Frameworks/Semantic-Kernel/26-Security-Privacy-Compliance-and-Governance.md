# 26 - Security, Privacy, Compliance, and Governance

## 26.1 Security Architecture
Protect model credentials, plugin endpoints, data stores, and orchestration channels.

## 26.2 Privacy Controls
Apply masking, minimization, retention, and deletion controls by data class.

## 26.3 Compliance Mapping
Map controls to legal and internal policy frameworks.

## 26.4 Governance Model
Assign ownership for prompts, plugins, models, and policy exceptions.

## 26.5 Real-Time Example
Regulated assistant enforces data residency and logs all high-risk function calls.
