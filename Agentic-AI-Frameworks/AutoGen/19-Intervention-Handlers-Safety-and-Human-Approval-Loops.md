# 19 - Intervention Handlers, Safety, and Human Approval Loops

## 19.1 Intervention Model
Intervention handlers intercept and modify execution when risk thresholds are triggered.

## 19.2 Human Approval Gates
Use manual checkpoints for financial, legal, and security-sensitive actions.

## 19.3 Policy Enforcement
Embed policy checks before tool execution and before final response release.

## 19.4 Escalation Workflow
Route ambiguous or high-impact cases to specialists and compliance reviewers.

## 19.5 Real-Time Example
Payments workflow pauses on high-value transfer and resumes only after approval.
