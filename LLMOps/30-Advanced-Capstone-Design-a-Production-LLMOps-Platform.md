# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Design a Production LLMOps Platform

## 30.1 Capstone Goal
Design an enterprise LLMOps platform with quality, safety, and reliability guarantees.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
Architecture diagram, control plane design, eval strategy, and SLO model.

## 30.3 Evaluation Rubric
Assess reliability, compliance, cost-efficiency, and operational maturity.

## 30.4 Failure Simulation
Include outage scenario, rollback strategy, and postmortem improvement plan.

## 30.5 Real-Time Example
Capstone platform supports governed multi-agent support automation at enterprise scale.
