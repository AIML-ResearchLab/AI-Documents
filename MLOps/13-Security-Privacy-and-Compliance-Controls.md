# 13 - Security, Privacy, and Compliance Controls

## 13.1 Security Baseline
Protect pipelines, model endpoints, and artifacts with least privilege.

## 13.2 Privacy Controls
Apply masking, retention, and deletion policies for sensitive data.

## 13.3 Compliance Mapping
Map controls to regulatory and internal governance requirements.

## 13.4 Auditability
Maintain immutable evidence for decisions and model changes.

## 13.5 Real-Time Example
Sensitive features are tokenized before training and inference processing.
