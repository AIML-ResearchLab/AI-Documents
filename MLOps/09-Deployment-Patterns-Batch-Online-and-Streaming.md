# 09 - Deployment Patterns: Batch, Online, and Streaming

## 9.1 Batch Inference
Best for periodic scoring with large volume and lower latency demands.

## 9.2 Online Serving
Supports low-latency request-response inference.

## 9.3 Streaming Inference
Handles continuous event flows with near-real-time predictions.

## 9.4 Pattern Selection
Choose based on SLA, cost, and freshness requirements.

## 9.5 Real-Time Example
Fraud detection combines online scoring with nightly batch recalibration.
