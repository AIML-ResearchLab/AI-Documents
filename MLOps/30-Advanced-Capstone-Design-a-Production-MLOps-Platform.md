# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Design a Production MLOps Platform

## 30.1 Capstone Goal
Design a resilient, governed, and scalable enterprise MLOps platform.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
Reference architecture, control plane design, reliability model, and KPI/SLO framework.

## 30.3 Evaluation Rubric
Assess quality assurance, governance depth, scalability, and operational readiness.

## 30.4 Failure Simulation
Include rollback, disaster recovery, and postmortem improvement workflow.

## 30.5 Real-Time Example
Capstone platform supports regulated real-time risk scoring with full audit traceability.
