# 14 - Cost Optimization and Resource Efficiency

## 14.1 Cost Drivers
Compute, storage, inference traffic, and retraining frequency.

## 14.2 Optimization Levers
Right-size infrastructure, schedule jobs efficiently, and optimize model footprint.

## 14.3 Resource Governance
Set budgets and chargeback policies per team or product.

## 14.4 Tradeoff Strategy
Balance performance improvements against incremental spend.

## 14.5 Real-Time Example
Autoscaling policy reduces idle inference cost without SLA impact.
