# 13 - Security, Governance, and Multi-Tenant Graph Design

## 13.1 Access Controls
Apply role-based and attribute-based controls at node, edge, and property levels.

## 13.2 Governance Controls
Define retention, lineage, consent, and audit policies for graph data.

## 13.3 Multi-Tenancy
Use graph-level isolation, subgraph partitioning, or namespace-based tenancy.

## 13.4 Compliance Readiness
Map controls to regulatory frameworks and internal policy standards.

## 13.5 Real-Time Example
Enterprise graph platform enforces tenant-isolated traversals for regulated departments.
