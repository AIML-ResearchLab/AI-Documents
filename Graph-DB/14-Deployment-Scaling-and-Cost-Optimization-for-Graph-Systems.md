# 14 - Deployment, Scaling, and Cost Optimization for Graph Systems

## 14.1 Deployment Modes
Choose managed, self-hosted, or hybrid based on compliance and latency requirements.

## 14.2 Scaling Strategy
Scale read replicas, partition hot subgraphs, and optimize traversal-heavy workloads.

## 14.3 Cost Levers
Optimize memory footprint, indexing strategy, storage tiers, and query plans.

## 14.4 Reliability Practices
Implement backups, failover, disaster recovery, and staged upgrades.

## 14.5 Real-Time Example
Graph workload cost drops after moving cold relationship history to lower-cost storage.
