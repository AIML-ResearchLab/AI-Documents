# 19 - Model-Based RL and Planning

## 19.1 Model-Based Idea
Learn or use transition model to plan actions before acting.

## 19.2 Benefits
Higher sample efficiency versus pure model-free methods.

## 19.3 Risks
Model bias can compound over planning horizons.

## 19.4 Real-Time Example
Learned environment model accelerates policy search in expensive simulator.
