# 10 - Actor-Critic Foundations

## 10.1 Actor and Critic Roles
Actor updates policy; critic estimates value/advantage.

## 10.2 Benefits
Combines policy optimization flexibility with lower-variance learning signals.

## 10.3 Stability Considerations
Target networks, normalization, and trust-region style constraints can help.

## 10.4 Real-Time Example
Actor-critic optimizes dynamic pricing decisions with continuous feedback.
