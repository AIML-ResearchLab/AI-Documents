# 25 - Safe RL, Constrained Optimization, and Risk

## 25.1 Safety Motivation
Many RL applications have hard constraints and unacceptable failure modes.

## 25.2 Methods
- constrained MDP approaches
- risk-sensitive objectives
- shielded policies

## 25.3 Evaluation
Track constraint violations and worst-case outcomes, not only average return.

## 25.4 Real-Time Example
Policy constrained to keep customer wait time below contractual threshold.
