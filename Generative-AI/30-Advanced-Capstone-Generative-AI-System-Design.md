# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Generative AI System Design

## 30.1 Objective
Design a production-grade generative AI system with safety, reliability, and cost controls.

## 30.2 Required Deliverables
- architecture and workflow design
- evaluation and red-team report
- release and rollback strategy
- monitoring and incident runbooks
- governance and compliance checklist

## 30.3 Rubric
technical quality, safety posture, operational maturity, efficiency, documentation quality.

## 30.4 Suggested Capstones
enterprise copilot, regulated-domain assistant, multimodal content platform.
