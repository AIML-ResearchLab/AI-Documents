# 30 - Advanced Capstone: Deep Learning System Design

## 30.1 Objective
Design a production deep learning system with end-to-end reliability and governance.

## 30.2 Deliverables
- architecture document
- training and evaluation specification
- deployment and rollback strategy
- monitoring and incident plan
- safety and compliance checklist

## 30.3 Rubric
- technical correctness
- robustness and safety
- operational maturity
- cost and latency efficiency
- documentation quality

## 30.4 Suggested Capstones
- real-time fraud detection
- multilingual support assistant
- multimodal document intelligence
- recommendation ranking platform
